Your step-by-step guide to CargoWise warehouse operations at Logistics Plus. Select a workflow below to begin.

Create a Receipt

Log inbound shipments and create Advanced Shipment Notices (ASNs) in CargoWise to track arriving warehouse inventory from suppliers and freight partners.

or

Create an Order

Process outbound orders and generate pick lists for warehouse fulfillment. Manage dispatch and confirm shipment details for customer deliveries.

or

Milestone Management

Capture ETA and Actual Delivery Date milestones on warehouse orders so tracking and customer visibility stay accurate through delivery.

Step 6 of 8

Create a Split (Partial Receipt)

If only part of the expected quantity arrives, split the receipt. Open the receipt and go to Actions → Split Receipt by Quantity. Enter the quantity actually received in the Split Quantity column on each line, then click Save. To open the split afterwards, go to the Related Splits tab and click the split row.

Step 7 of 8

Import Serials

Load the serial numbers into the receipt lines:

  1. Save the serial-number Excel file as a CSV.
  2. Right-click the receipt lines grid and choose Import Data…
  3. In the Data Import Wizard's Mapping tab, drag each CSV column onto its corresponding CargoWise field.

Step 7 of 14

Generate a Picking Slip

From the Edit Pick screen, open the Documents menu and select Picking Slip. The Deliver Documents box pops up — click Preview to open the slip. Print it and follow the QC procedure below while picking:

  1. Mark if any SKU needs to be Asset tagged or have its Serial number captured.
  2. Pick items and enter the Quantity picked on the slip.
  3. A different staff member QCs all products on the pick and signs with initials (Picked By / Checked By boxes).

Step 13 of 14

Capture Serials / Asset Tags

Capture serial numbers / asset tags at release:

  1. Type something in the Serial number field on the release line.
  2. Right-click and choose Import Data…
  3. Browse to the CSV file you created with all the serials.
  4. In the Mapping tab, click and drag each CSV field to its corresponding CargoWise field (e.g. Sage Serial Numbers, Asset Tag).

Step 1 of 2

Capture the ETA

The ETA must be populated when/before an order is finalized. Open the order's Workflow & Tracking tab → Milestones, and enter the ETA in the Estimated Date column on the Delivered milestone row.

cargowise.logisticsplus.com / Edit Order / Workflow & Tracking
Screenshot needed CW_MILESTONES_ETA.png

An order's Workflow & Tracking tab → Milestones sub-tab, showing the Shipped and Delivered milestone rows. Red box + arrow on the empty Estimated Date cell with an "ETA" callout.

The ETA must be populated when — or before — an order is Finalized.
Step 2 of 2

Capture the Actual Delivery Date

When delivery is confirmed, return to Workflow & Tracking → Milestones and record the date in the Actual Start column on the Delivered milestone row.

Same Milestones screen: red boxes on the Workflow & Tracking tab and Milestones sub-tab at the top, and on the populated date in the Actual Start column, with an "Actual Delivery Date" callout.

The actual delivery date must be entered once delivery is confirmed.
